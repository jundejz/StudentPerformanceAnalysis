Conclusion

From our analysis, we can see that:

  • Females perform better than males on average.
  • As parental education level becomes higher, the averages scores also increase for both males and females
  • Students who received free or reduced lunches performed worse on average than those who did not, for both males and females.
  • Students who undertook test preparation performed better on average than those who did not, for both males and females.
Consequently, we should aim to provide more economic support to those students who receive free or reduced lunches and try to ensure everyone has adequate test preparation help.
